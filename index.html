Takeout your media, on your own terms, with free and open source software

Takeout is a free and open source media system that allows you manage and stream your media on your own terms. You can store your media at home or in the cloud, run the server anywhere, and create a media system that works best for you. Enjoy your media on your phone, desktop, television, and watch. All using free software, without any restrictions or limits. Any fees for servers, media, storage, and egress are decided by you.
